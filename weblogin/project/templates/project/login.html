How to Use this platform?

  1. For patients/ patient's relative:
    You can find the list of oxygen suppliers and plasma donors by clicking on links above. If your requirement is still not met, please register/login and post your request. We will try to reach out to you as soon as possible.
  2. For Oxygen Suppliers:
    Please login (if you're already registered on this website)/ register (if you're a new user), fill out the form for oxygen supplier and submit. Your request will be posted in List of Oxygen Suppliers.
  3. For Plasma Donors:
    Please login (if you're already registered on this website)/ register (if you're a new user), fill out the form for plasmna donor and submit. Your request will be posted in List of Plasma Donors.
  4. For login, use your username and password. To register (if you're a new user), click on register and fill out: username, email id, password. Once you are logged in /registered, you will be tasken to a dashboard automatically where you can fill out form depending upon you requiremnet/role.
